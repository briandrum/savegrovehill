LATEST: Developer Robert Vitt still owns 3 W Summit, having refused all known offers to purchase and preserve the Bancroft Estate. On February 20 he withdrew a proposal to DEMOLISH the Bancroft home and accessory building. As of Feb 21 the property is not on the Architectural Board of Review agenda.

Chagrin is on the verge of losing its iconic Grove Hill

At a November 19th meeting of the town’s Architectural Review Board, the new owner of the property on the southwest corner of Grove Hill – developer Robert Vitt – announced plans to…

  • Demolish the historic Bancroft House that sits at the crest of the property and its accessory building to make way to…
  • Construct two new homes facing West Summit Street, and…
  • Remove 1,000 tons of soil from the top of Grove Hill to “stabilize” the slope to…
  • Construct three new townhomes near the bottom of the property facing West Cottage Street.

So what can you do? Learn more about what’s planned. Show up to ARB meetings. Get in touch with other concerned residents.
