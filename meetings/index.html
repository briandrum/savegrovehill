Meetings

The Architectural Board of Review meets on the first and third Tuesday of each month at 8:30 AM.

The Board of Zoning Appeals meets on the fourth Tuesday of each month at 8:00 PM.

Meetings are held in the Council Chamber at the Village Hall, 21 West Washington Street.
